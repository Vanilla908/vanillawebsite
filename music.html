My Music Albums

1. The Pocket Orchestra

The following is my first collection of songs I made while experimenting on a DAW program known as LMMS. Most of what is made here is made for the purposes of learning how to make music while applying the lessons I learned on youtube involving music theory, audio engineering, and unique tools I found in LMMS.

2. Medical Students

After 9 months of making music in a DAW Program, I made 14 original soundtracks inspired by my years studying as a dental student. The aim of the album is to give the experience of studying in a medical field; from its innocent first few years studying basic subjects that revisit the lessons you were taught in high school to the stressful final years you have as a clinician working overtime. People will come and go, beautiful moments will happen as well as moments of hardship, and you will fundamentally question the idea of when the transition between student and doctor truly begins (Or maybe it does not even happen at all).
